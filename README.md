## English

I build AI automation systems for businesses across Canada, the United States
and Latin America — working natively in **English and Spanish**.

### Why bilingual matters

Most businesses in North America serve customers in more than one language.
Instead of running two separate systems, I build one that detects the
customer's language and responds naturally in it — same quality, same brand
voice, no translation layer.

### What I do

- 🤖 Conversational AI sales agents on WhatsApp
- 🔄 Process automation with n8n
- 📚 RAG systems over private knowledge bases
- 📊 Data, CRM and follow-up integration
- 🌐 Bilingual deployments (EN / ES) from day one
